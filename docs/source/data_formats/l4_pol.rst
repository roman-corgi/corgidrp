L4 Polarimetry Data Product
========================================


FITS HDU Structure
------------------


+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
| Index | Name             | Datatype | Array Size           |
+=======+==================+==========+======================+
| 0     | Primary          | None     | 0                    |
+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
| 1     | Image            | float64  | (4, 54, 54)          |
+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
| 2     | ERR              | float64  | (1, 4, 54, 54)       |
+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
| 3     | DQ               | uint16   | (4, 54, 54)          |
+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
| 4     | KL_THRU          | float64  | (2, 2, 2)            |
+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
| 5     | CT_THRU          | float64  | (2, 2)               |
+-------+------------------+----------+----------------------+
